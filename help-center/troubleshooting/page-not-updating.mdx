---
title: Page Not Updating
description: Fix issues when Chamely pages don't save changes.
---

## Symptoms

- Edited pages show old content
- Changes are not reflected after save

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    ````tip
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to force refresh.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````info
    Open browser settings > Clear cache > Restart browser.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````info
    Unstable connection can prevent changes from saving.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
