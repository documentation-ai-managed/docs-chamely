## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Access to your email inbox for confirmation links

No software installation required - Chamely runs entirely in your browser.

</Callout>

## Create Your Chamely Account

Follow these steps to sign up and log in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://chamely.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and fill in your name.
    
    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Chamely.
    
    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://chamely.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  
You can also sign up using Google or GitHub for faster onboarding.

</Callout>

## Create a New Documentation Space

A documentation space organizes all your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    After logging in, you land on the Chamely dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click the **New Space** button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, add a description, and select visibility (public or private).
    
    Click **Create Space**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Page

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit-3">
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Page">
        In your space, click **New Page**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Edit Content">
        Use the visual editor to add headings, lists, and images.
        
        Drag and drop components like cards or callouts.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="MDX Editor" icon="code">
    
    Switch to MDX mode for advanced formatting.
    
````mdx
---

title: My First Page
description: Welcome to my documentation.

---

## Introduction

This is your first MDX page in Chamely.

<Callout kind="success">

Great job creating your first doc!

</Callout>
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Preview and Publish

Test your content before going live.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview" icon="eye">
    Click the **Preview** button to see the live version.
    
    Make adjustments as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload-cloud">
    Once satisfied, click **Publish**.
    
    Your page is now live at a shareable URL like `https://chamely.com/spaces/my-project-docs/my-first-page`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share" icon="share-2">
    Copy the URL or use the share modal to invite collaborators.
  </Step>
</Steps>
